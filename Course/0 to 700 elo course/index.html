0 to 700 Elo Course





The course is organised by Zawata Afnan and Mujahedul Anwar. This three players are an intermediate. They have 1000+ rating in chess.com. Our main goal is to improve your playing quality. The course is designed for beginners. Also a player who is new to chess can also continue with this course. There are 30 lessons. 1-15 lessons is for new to chess player and 15-30 lessons is for beginners. If you learn 1 lesson everyday, we hope you can gain 700 elo in 1 month. There is 1 puzzle given with each lesson from lesson - 5. Play the quiz for advancing your playing quality. If you can't understand that puzzle, then there is a option of 'learn with sense'. This course is totally free. We hope you will enjoy our course and complete your goal!
